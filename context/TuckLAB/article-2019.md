# Tuck Launches Bold New Program for Dartmouth Undergraduates

**Source:** https://tuck.dartmouth.edu/news/articles/tuck-launches-bold-entrepreneurship-program-for-dartmouth-undergraduates
**Date:** February 5, 2019
**Author:** Kristin Maffei
**Archived on:** January 2026

---

## Overview

Tuck School of Business and Dartmouth's Magnuson Center for Entrepreneurship partnered to create TuckLAB, a six-week program designed to equip undergraduate students with business analytics, communication skills, and entrepreneurship knowledge applicable to any career path.

## Key Details

- **Program Duration:** Six weeks
- **Faculty:** Six Tuck professors and one Thayer professor
- **Structure:** Divided into Core (fundamental business skills) and ABLE Concentration (Applied Business Learning and Entrepreneurship)
- **Capstone:** "Shark Tank"-style pitch competition
- **Application Response:** 130 applications for 60 spots; second cohort approved for March 30

## Founders

Rick Magnuson D'79, Jeffrey Crowe D'78, Dean Matthew J. Slaughter, Deputy Dean Punam Anand Keller, and Jamie Coughlin (Magnuson Center director)
