How it works

Three steps. No setup.

From "I should start" to "I'm presenting" takes about fifteen seconds. Pin, scan, swipe.

1

Pin the extension

Open your Google Slides and click the cliko icon in Chrome. A QR code appears.

2

Scan it with your phone

Your phone opens vinicius.pm/cliko. Tap one button. A room is born.

3

Swipe or tap

Swipe left for next. Right for back. Or tap either side. That's the whole thing.
